A memory left forgotten...

Days turned into weeks, turned into months, turned into years.

The world I had become a part of, something so alien to me, became my every day. I lost the part of me that connected to my past.

At the same time, I made no effort to rekindle those memories.

Was it fear? Was it hatred? Was it sorrow?

My mind slowly became a part of the world, as I took form in this world so different to me.

Names I hade once seen in prophetic visions became friends and acquaintences.

But I never forgot how it all came to end.
