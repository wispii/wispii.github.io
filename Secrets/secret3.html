A love made bare...

As the close finally came, memories rushed back to me.

The friends I had made, who stood by me, and those who brought me to this world held hands with me.

I knew what it took to stop the end, but it was only now, after sharing my heart with them, could I truly let go of who I was before, and embrace who I am.

I stopped the end, but I forgot who I used to be.

Thank you, my friends at Caldera Bay, for letting this divine dreamer finally open her eyes.
