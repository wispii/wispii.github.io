A secret left untold...

My mind drifted through the endless eternity. One vision after another. I saw worlds my mind could only glimpse at.

never truly feeling.
never truly knowing.

I was awake, but I felt like I was dreaming.
I saw beginnings. I saw endings.

My mind drifting from world to world, like embers in the wind.

One world, so close to the end, took my mind hold.
No matter how often I wished for the dream to end another way, I was unable to change things before the close.

With all my strength, with all of my wishing and all of my tears, I pierced the veil, but when I awoke, I had forgotten who I was.

In wishing to become real, I had lost divinity. I traded my happy life for a real life.

I forgotten the names of those I cried for. But I would do so again.
